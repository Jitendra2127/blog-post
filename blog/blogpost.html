‘Future Thinking’ is a series of books about Technology, Trends, Business and the environment at large. Jean-Pierre discusses both local and global matters influencing the trajectory of companies, communities and our human civilization. It is an easy to read book, highly topical and will inspire ‘Change Champions’.

This latest addition covers:

Wiser decisions, Climate Change, Several Trends and Innovations, ‘things’ about Zimbabwe, e-sports and an interview with Tendai Mtawarira, Hypocrites, E-security, ‘Waiting for Racists to die?’, Digital Copyright Infringements, E-health, China, E-Behaviour and a Chat with Chester Missing, Sustainable and Ethical Future Businesses and people, Automation & I4.0, types of jobs for The Future, Space Colonization, 5G, Smart Cities, Rwanda, Natural Disasters, Lessons from Covid, Predicting the Future, and to end off we discuss Nature.
